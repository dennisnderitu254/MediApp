How to get a Doctor

We are here to ensure a smooth onboarding

  • Registration

    create account and login

    To get started, click on the "Sign Up" button located at the top right corner of the page. Fill in the required information, such as your name, email address, and password. Ensure your password meets the specified criteria for security. Once completed, click "Create Account" to register successfully.

  • Choose a service

    Browse through our list of available medical consultation services on the homepage or the "Services" section. You can use the search bar or filter options to narrow down your choices based on specialties, doctors, or specific conditions. Click on the service of your choice to view more details, including doctor profiles and availability.

  • Schedule an appointment

    After selecting a service, you'll be directed to the appointment scheduling page. Choose a convenient date and time for your consultation from the available slots displayed. If you prefer a specific doctor, you can select them at this stage as well. Once you've made your selections, click "Confirm" to proceed to the next step.

  • Provide medical history

    To ensure the doctor has all the necessary information, we kindly request that you provide your medical history before the consultation. You'll be prompted to fill out a form where you can enter details about your current and past medical conditions, medications, allergies, and any other relevant information. Rest assured that your information is treated with strict confidentiality.

  • Complete payment

    For certain services, there may be a payment requirement. If applicable, you'll be guided to a secure payment page where you can choose your preferred payment method, such as credit card or PayPal. Follow the prompts to complete the payment process. Once the payment is successful, you'll receive a confirmation of your payment.

General Medical Consultation

Get quality consultation from our seasoned doctors

Our general medicine consultation service provides comprehensive healthcare for a wide range of common health issues and concerns. Our team of experienced doctors specializes in internal medicine and is dedicated to delivering high-quality care. During your consultation, our doctors will conduct a thorough assessment, taking into account your medical history, symptoms, and any relevant diagnostic tests. They will carefully listen to your concerns, ask detailed questions, and perform a physical examination to gain a comprehensive understanding of your condition.

Following the assessment, our doctors will provide an accurate diagnosis and explain the findings in a clear and understandable manner. They will discuss the available treatment options, including medication, lifestyle modifications, and necessary referrals to specialists if required. Our goal is to empower you with knowledge about your health and involve you in the decision-making process, ensuring that your treatment plan aligns with your preferences and individual needs.

Additionally, our general medicine consultation service places a strong emphasis on preventive care. Our doctors will work with you to develop personalized preventive strategies based on your medical history, risk factors, and lifestyle. This may include recommendations for routine screenings, vaccinations, healthy lifestyle habits, and disease prevention. By focusing on both acute and preventive care, we strive to optimize your overall health and well-being, enabling you to lead a fulfilling and active life.

Specialist referrals

Get refered to top specialist

Our specialist referral service ensures that you receive the specialized care you need for complex or specific medical conditions. Our team of doctors includes experts in various medical specialties, each with extensive experience and expertise in their respective fields. If your symptoms or condition require specialized evaluation or treatment, our doctors will conduct a comprehensive assessment and recommend the most suitable specialist for your specific needs.

We understand that navigating the healthcare system and finding the right specialist can be challenging. That's why we take pride in our seamless referral process. Our team will handle all the necessary coordination, including scheduling appointments, sharing medical records, and communicating with the specialist on your behalf. We maintain close relationships with a network of trusted specialists, ensuring that you receive the highest standard of care throughout your treatment journey.

Whether you require a cardiologist, dermatologist, orthopedic surgeon, neurologist, or any other specialized care, our specialist referral service streamlines the process, minimizing delays and ensuring timely access to the expertise you need. Our doctors will continue to collaborate with the specialist, providing comprehensive and integrated care to address your specific medical condition. We are committed to supporting you at every step, ensuring that you receive the specialized attention and treatment necessary to optimize your health outcomes.

Telemedicine consultation

Easily communicate with your favourite doctor online.

Our telemedicine consultation service revolutionizes access to healthcare by bringing our experienced doctors directly to you, wherever you are. Through secure and encrypted video conferencing technology, you can connect with our doctors from the comfort and convenience of your home or any location with an internet connection. This eliminates the need for travel, reduces wait times, and offers a flexible solution for non-emergency medical consultations.

During a telemedicine consultation, our doctors will engage in a thorough discussion to understand your symptoms, medical history, and concerns. They will conduct a virtual examination, guiding you through self-assessment techniques when necessary. By leveraging advanced technology, our doctors can visually assess physical symptoms, review any available medical reports, and provide accurate diagnoses.

Once the diagnosis is determined, our doctors will create a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs. This may involve prescribing medications, recommending lifestyle modifications, providing guidance on self-care measures, or suggesting further diagnostic tests if required. Our telemedicine consultation service is suitable for a wide range of conditions, including follow-up visits, medication management, minor acute illnesses, chronic disease management, mental health support, and general medical advice. Rest assured that your privacy and confidentiality are of utmost importance to us, and all telemedicine consultations are conducted in a secure and HIPAA-compliant environment.

Second Opinion

Get a second opinion from a different specialist

Our second opinion consultation service offers you the opportunity to seek an additional expert evaluation of your medical condition. We understand that receiving a complex or critical diagnosis can be overwhelming, and making informed decisions about your health is crucial. Our team of specialized doctors will thoroughly review your medical history, test results, and diagnostic reports to provide an unbiased and comprehensive assessment.

During a second opinion consultation, our doctors will take the time to understand your concerns, answer any questions you may have, and provide clarity on your diagnosis and treatment options. They will offer a fresh perspective, drawing upon their extensive expertise and knowledge in their respective fields. Our goal is to empower you with the information needed to make confident decisions about your healthcare journey.

We believe in the importance of collaboration and patient autonomy. Our second opinion consultation service is designed to complement your existing healthcare team, ensuring that you have all the necessary information to make informed decisions. Whether you seek clarification, alternative treatment options, or reassurance, our doctors are committed to providing you with a thorough and objective assessment. We understand the significance of second opinions in healthcare decision-making and are dedicated to supporting you throughout the process.

Mental Health Consultation

Get access to mental health experts

Our mental health consultation service is centered around providing compassionate support and guidance for your mental well-being. Our team of mental health professionals, including psychiatrists and psychologists, specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of various mental health conditions. We recognize the importance of mental health in overall well-being and offer a safe and empathetic space for you to discuss your concerns.

During a mental health consultation, our experts will conduct a comprehensive assessment to understand your emotional, psychological, and behavioral patterns. They will listen attentively to your experiences, evaluate symptoms, and provide an accurate diagnosis. Our approach to treatment is tailored to your specific needs and may include therapy, medication management, lifestyle adjustments, and coping strategies.

We believe in a holistic and patient-centered approach to mental health. Our mental health consultation service is not just focused on symptom management but also emphasizes promoting overall well-being and improving quality of life. Our dedicated team of mental health professionals will work closely with you to develop a personalized treatment plan, providing ongoing support, guidance, and education. Our goal is to empower you to overcome challenges, build resilience, and achieve better mental health and emotional balance.

Lifestyle and preventive health Consultation

Connect with our seasoned doctors and get the best advice

Our lifestyle and preventive health consultation service is designed to help you lead a healthy and fulfilling life by prioritizing preventive care and making positive lifestyle choices. Our doctors will conduct a comprehensive assessment of your medical history, lifestyle habits, and family background to develop personalized plans tailored to your needs.

During a lifestyle and preventive health consultation, our doctors will provide guidance on nutrition, exercise, stress management, and preventive screenings. They will help you understand your risk factors for various diseases and work with you to develop strategies to mitigate those risks. Our approach focuses on empowering you to take control of your health through informed decision-making and adopting healthy habits.

Our lifestyle and preventive health consultation service aims to prevent future health issues and promote overall well-being. By addressing risk factors, implementing healthy lifestyle changes, and emphasizing preventive measures, we strive to improve your quality of life and support you in achieving long-term health goals. Our doctors will provide ongoing support, monitor your progress, and adjust the treatment plan as needed, ensuring that you have the necessary tools to maintain a healthy and balanced lifestyle.
